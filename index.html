Bio

Matt and Kim are an indie electro duo made up of two members, Matt Johnson as vocals/keyboard, and Kim Schifino as drums. They started performing in 2004 out of Brooklyn, NY and exploded in popularity from there. The pair are often considered to make an upbeat dance-pop or electronic-pop style of music, and their songs are occasionally used in both films and advertisement. They are also a romantically involved couple and known widely for their eccentric and energetic live performances, often incorporating props and samples from other artists into their sets. They are extremely influential in their genre of music.

Although they started out playing in lofts and house shows, they now frequent multiple music festivals and constantly play around the US. Since the start of the duo, they have released 6 studio albums. Which include the following:

  • ALMOST EVERYDAY (2018)
  • New Glow (2015)
  • Lightning Remixes (2013)
  • Lightning (2012)
  • Sidewalks (2010)
  • Grand (2009)
